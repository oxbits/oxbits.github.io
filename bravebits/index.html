  • click on the magnifying glass tool:

  • click in the image area will zoom in
  • option click[mac] / Alt click[pc] will zoom out

[keyboard key mouse click combination]

  • click on the brush size editor button:

  • first select the square brush [with the 20 under it] and then slide the "Size" down to "1 px" [see configuration in image below]:

  • click the foreground color selection button:
    [orange rounded square in image below]

  • click "OK"

  • make sure you have the "Background" layer selected [clicked]:

[toggle "✓Layers" on under "Window" if you do not see it]

  • draw on your image:

if you make a mistake you can use "Step Backward":

[keyboard shortcut: command[mac]/Ctrl[pc] Z]

first select the square brush [with the 20 under it] then slide the size down to "1 px" [see configuration in image below]:
